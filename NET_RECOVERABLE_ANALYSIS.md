# Net Recoverable Value Analysis

## Core Formula
**Net Recoverable = [Gross Revenue] - [Selling Costs & Fees]**

## Your $5 Fallback Scenario - Detailed Breakdown

### Unit Distribution
- **Known Items (eBay matched):** 122 SKUs = 250 units
- **Unknown Items (no eBay data):** 100 SKUs = 177 units
- **Total Units:** 427

### Step 1: Gross Revenue Calculation
- Known Items Revenue: $10,472.50 (250 units × eBay average prices)
- Fallback Items Revenue: $885.00 (177 units × $5.00)
- **Total Projected Gross Revenue: $11,357.50**

### Step 2: Selling Costs & Fees (20% deduction)
- eBay Final Value Fee: 12.9%
- eBay Payment Processing: 3.49% + $0.30
- Shipping/Handling: ~3.5%
- **Total Fees: ~20% = $2,271.50**

### Step 3: Net Recoverable Value
- **Net Recoverable = $11,357.50 - $2,271.50 = $9,086.00**

### Step 4: Max Bid (100% ROI Target)
- **Standard Bid = $9,086.00 ÷ 2 = $4,543.00**
- **Conservative Bid (150% ROI) = $9,086.00 ÷ 3 = $3,029.00**

- **Clean Net Recoverable: $5,577.32**
- **Safe Max Bid (100% ROI): $2,788.66**
